# Paints Well Time Clock — Privacy Policy

**Effective July 21, 2026**

This app is a private employee time clock operated by Paints Well with Colors, Billings, MT. It is used only by our employees and bookkeeper.

**What we collect:** your name and email (to create your account), and your device's location at the moment you clock in or clock out (stored with that punch). Punches may include a job address and pay type chosen at clock-in.

**Why:** to keep accurate, verifiable work-hour records for payroll.

**Who sees it:** the company owner and bookkeeper. We do not sell or share your information with anyone else. Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) and encrypted in transit.

**Location:** captured only at the moment of a punch — the app does not track you continuously and records nothing while you are clocked out or off the app.

**Retention and deletion:** punch records are kept as payroll records. When an employee leaves, their sign-in is removed. To request deletion of other data, contact the owner: tcanderson23@gmail.com / (406) 530-1200.
